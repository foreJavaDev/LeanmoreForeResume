Software Engineer Who Builds for Production

I'm a results-driven software engineer with hands-on experience designing, developing, and deploying production-grade applications — from scalable school management platforms to health-tech systems serving real users. I don't just write code; I architect solutions that solve business problems end-to-end.

Currently pursuing a B.Sc. in Computer Science at the University of the People (USA), I combine strong academic foundations with battle-tested engineering skills across the full stack: Spring Boot and .NET on the backend, Django and Laravel for rapid delivery, and Python-powered machine learning for intelligent features.
